Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Influnet.

Are there any joining fees? +
Influnet offers flexible plans for businesses and creators. Business plans start with a free tier for discovery, with premium features for advanced matching and campaign management. Creator accounts are free to join.
How are creators and businesses verified? +
Creators undergo social platform verification and audience/engagement review. Businesses complete profile validation and identity confirmation before accessing the platform.
Can I chat directly with creators or brands? +
Yes. Influnet includes built-in direct messaging so you can manage all collaboration conversations in one secure place without switching to external tools.
Are small influencers eligible to join? +
Absolutely. Influnet welcomes creators of all sizes — from micro-influencers with engaged niche audiences to creators with millions of followers. Quality and authenticity matter more than follower count.
